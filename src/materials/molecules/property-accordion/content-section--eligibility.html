Ellis Act Preference {{> icons.icon-arrow}}

Ellis Act Housing Preference holders are primarily households displaced by Ellis Act evictions as defined by The Ellis Act Displacement Emergency Assistance Ordinance passed into law on December 18, 2013.

Live/Work Preference {{> icons.icon-arrow}}

Households that live or work in San Francisco will be given third highest preference in the lottery ranking process.

Rental Assistance

Vouchers and other valid rental assistance programs can be used to rent a BMR unit.

Subsidies and Vouchers {{> icons.icon-arrow}}

Section 8 housing vouchers and other valid rental assistance programs can be used to rent a BMR unit. In the case of a valid rent subsidy, the required minimum income will be based on the portion of the rent that the tenant pays after the use of the subsidy.
